🌐 Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 27, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how RushKaro ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website (rushkaro.fun) or mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Info: Name, Email, Mobile, Game ID, UPI, etc.
  • Device Info: Model, OS, IP, device ID, etc.
  • Usage: Matches played, rewards claimed, etc.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • For tournament participation & user identification
  • To process payments, rewards, and referrals
  • To send updates and important notifications
  • To prevent fraud & enhance gameplay experience

3. Sharing of Data

We never sell or rent your data. We only share with verified services like:

  • UPI Payment Processors
  • Email/WhatsApp notification providers

4. Data Security

We use SSL encryption, secure databases, and access control to protect user data. Users are advised not to share passwords or OTPs with anyone.

5. User Rights

  • Request access or deletion of your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account anytime via support

6. Children’s Privacy

RushKaro is not for users under 13. If you're under 13, use only under guardian supervision.

7. Cookies & Tracking

Cookies may be used for session management, analytics, or preferences. You can disable them in browser settings.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update our privacy practices. All users will be informed of major changes via app or website notice.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions, contact us at:
